A bunch more minor tweaks!

  • Tutorial is now excluded from the school end time because it's effectively optional.
  • Period 8 is now disabled by default to reduce confusion. If you have a period 8, turn it back on in Options.
  • PSAs won't be marked as read when UGWA auto-updates.
  • The alternate schedules stored offline has been reset, so last year's alternate schedules don't prop up this year and show school on Saturday.
  • The normal schedule will show as a placeholder while alternate schedules load.
  • Notifications at the start of a period will open the period's associated link when clicked.
  • Empty assignments are editable now.
  • Fixed colours not showing properly in the assignment editor's period dropdown.
  • There's now a hint on how to stop viewing a barcode just in case.
